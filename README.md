# 📖 You Are a Badass — Reading Journal

Welcome to my reading journal for **_You Are a Badass_** by **Jen Sincero**.

This repository documents my daily reading progress, observations, new vocabulary, and personal reflections as I work through the book.
Rather than summarizing each chapter, I focus on the ideas that stood out to me and how they relate to my own life.

## 🎯 Goals

- Build a consistent daily reading habit.
- Improve my English vocabulary.
- Practice critical thinking and reflection.
- Keep a record of my personal growth.

## 📝 Each Entry Includes

- 📅 Date
- 📄 Pages Read
- 🧐 What I Observed/What I Learned 
- 📖 New Words
- 💭 My Thoughts
